Herogotchi

Your's little hero journey start here

You take care of a teenager from the 13th century, you set a goal for yourself, it can be like trying to write his/her life in history, or maybe you will try to live as long as possible?!

After pressing the button to add an adventurer person_add, his/her life cycle begins immediately.

Every two seconds, the avatar becomes elder and loses a small amount of all stats and pays the cost of living in the amount of 2 coins, if the required amount is not available, the avatar will become a beggar, which will cause a significant decrease in stats, so keep an eye on the level of finances.

    There are three main methods of obtaining finance:
  • 1) Working on a farm grass is the easiest way, where you spend strength and happiness to get a small compensation in money and food;
  • 2) Working at a forge construction - consumes a significant part of health, strength, saturation and happiness, but you get a decent salary;
  • 3) Go on an adventure swords - the most dangerous venture where you spend a large part of strength, saturation and health in exchange for a small monetary reward and an increase in happiness, but this is the only way to get fame and write the life of the avatar in history.

Maintaining vital indicators is the main problem of the game, when any indicator drops to zero, the avatar dies (gets a terrible disease, gets fatally overworked, dies of hunger or commits suicide).

Health increases while feeding restaurant_menu.

Strength increases while resting self_improvement in exchange for saturation.

Saturation can be raised by feeding restaurant_menu, or maintained by working on the farm grass.

Happiness can be obtained either by visiting a brothel hotel or by going on an adventure swords.

Feeding restaurant_menu costs 10 coins, visiting a brothel hotel costs 20 so sometimes it wouldn't be available.

Remember: adventurers live short, but brightly and make history, while others live long but unhappy lives.
